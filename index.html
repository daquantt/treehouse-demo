About Us

Treehouse Enterprises (formerly known as Pure Honey) started off with a passion for understanding the amazing works of bees and how they function in their magical buzzing world. Many bee stings later, this passion blossomed into a blooming family business, operating for over four decades with beehives tucked far away in the untouched bushes of Clear Water, Rio Claro.

Our Africanized honey bees, more popularly known as "killer bees," forage on a variety of nectarine sources. These include Mora, Savonette, Cocoa, Coffee, Soap Vine, and the magnificent Immortelle to name a few. This leads to a unique exotic tropical medley of natural flavours - the way nature intended for us!

Curated by beekeeper Dhanpat, Treehouse Enterprises bring to you a range of products such as pure honey (absolutely no additives!), cinnamon honey, honey sticks, honeycomb, beeswax candles infused with essential oils and much more!
